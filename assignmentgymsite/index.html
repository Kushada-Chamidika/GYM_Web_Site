About Us..

     Health First Fitness offers an atmosphere unique in the fitness industry. We focus on creating healthy lifestyles through all disciplines of fitness training. Our clientele will have access to different types of group exercise classes and will be heavily encouraged to participate in as many as possible. Our clientele will learn how to create their own path to wellness using disciplines such as boot-camp style training, yoga, weightlifting, martial arts, suspension training, athlete training and combination classes. Health First offers an environment that is the truest embodiment of CROSS TRAINING available under one roof.

Our Vision..

     At Health First Fitness Center, we want to provide you with the most resources possible to reach your fitness goals. As a member of our gym, you have the option for a consultation once a month with one of our owners. This consultation includes a full fitness assessment, and a conversation with you to help set fitness goals, go over your activity level, current activities, and active hobbies. We will then build a curriculum of classes for you to take each month based on all of those factors. The curriculum will be designed specifically toward YOU and your goals to help achieve maximum results in minimal time.
